Risk
=======

A risk may be associated with many objectives (many to many relation). When creating a risk, it has to be associated to atleast one :doc:`objective`.

Each risk can have many :doc:`control` s.

A risk has five basic aspects:

* Title
   This field has a maximum of 200 characters.
* Reference
   This field has a maximum of 200 characters.
* Description
   This is a regular text field where there is no limit to the number of characters.
* Objective
   A risk has to be associated to atleast one :doc:`objective`.
* Work papers
   Work papers are attachments or file uploads where you can keep files associated to a risk.
