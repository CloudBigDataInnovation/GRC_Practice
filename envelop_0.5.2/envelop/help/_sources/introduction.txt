Introduction to GRC Envelop
===========================


GRC Envelop is a web-based work flow and document storage tool specific for the **risk**, **audit** and **control environments**. This tool is mainly used for internal and external audits focused on financial, IT, HR and sales processes within firms.

This tools falls under a broad category of services called GRC which stands for governance, risk and compliance.

Envelop is modelled using a basic workflow that starts from a business :doc:`audits/process`.
